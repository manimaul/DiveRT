DiveRT was created for Bering SubSea Mining and the crews aboard Norpac, Tahta and Julianna.  Use of DiveRT without the expressed permission from Will Kamp is prohibited.

DiveRT records diver and tender dive times, position and bearing data into a sqlite3 database
Connects to Nema0183 GPS and bearing sensor (reads HDT,HDG, RMC, GGA data)
